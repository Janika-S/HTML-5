Red-ocher Painted Riverside Warehouses

In honour of King Gustav III's arrival all of the houses along his route were painted to make them look more beatiful. That is why the riverside warehouses were painted with red ochre paint in the late 18th century. The red ochre paint did not only make the warehouses look more beatiful but also helped protect the logs from wind and sun damages.
